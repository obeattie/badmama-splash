Bad Mama

Because you had a baby, not a lobotomy.

Mother and baby groups not for you? More interested in a martini than another round of The Wheels on the Bus? Here at Bad Mama we believe that you don't stop needing glamour and excitement just because you've become a mum. And regularly. There are a thousand and one opinions on How To Be A Good Mum – it's a thorny old business these days and we'd like to balance it out by helping you to remain the fabulous creature you are. Post partum doesn't have to mean post fun.

If you're a Bad Mama and you want to meet other likeminded women, enjoy fabulous events, network, set up businesses, get a make over, even go on fabulous holidays – with and without your little ones and other halves then this club is for you. The rules – no baggy old clothes, best underwear at all times (even if no one sees it), plenty of attitude, plenty of laughter and a new kind of motherhood for the 21st century…
